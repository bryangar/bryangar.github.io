About Me


I always thought my dream was to be an accountant in one of the Big 4s as that seemed to be the obvious career path for accounting students. Not until I arrive in Melbourne 4 years ago, that I began to uncover my personal aspirations and took on other career choices.

Never would I have imagined myself to be sitting behind my laptop all day coding to get a website up to speed. Since I began my Bachelor in IT, major in software development, I could not stop myself from diving deeper into programming languages, data structures, security threathing ,and graphic design. My curiosity got the better of me and I did my first freelance job to help someone to build his professional website just a week into learning programming.

I am the colleague which everyone would look to when they need an answer. It brings a huge joy to me knowing that I am able to enrich someone’s life or help someone in need. I enjoy making new friends and hearing new perspectives. I am highly inquisitive and constantly searching for new things to learn and challenge myself.

I would like to to enrich my knowledge on IT because that is where my passion lies in. If you are interested to know more about me and my skills, feel free to reach out.
